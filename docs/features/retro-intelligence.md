## The Problem

Traditional retrospectives rely on **what people remember** — which is biased, incomplete, and often dominated by the loudest voice in the room. "What went well?" gets generic answers. "What could improve?" turns into a complaint session. Action items rarely get followed up.

---

## The Solution

<div class="screenshot" markdown>
![Retro Intelligence — AI-generated retrospective analysis](../assets/screens/flavors/pm/retro-intelligence.png)
</div>

Retro Intelligence pre-generates a **data-driven retrospective** before the meeting even starts. JP looks at what actually happened — commit patterns, PR cycle times, blocker durations, sprint completion rates — and structures it into actionable insights.

!!! success "Impact"
    **Retros go from 60-minute vent sessions to 20-minute action-planning meetings.** Every insight is grounded in data your team can verify.
