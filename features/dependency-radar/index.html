    Dependency Radar¶

    See what blocks what — before it blocks you. Dependency Radar creates a visual map of cross-project dependencies, upstream/downstream relationships, and critical-path items. No more discovering dependency conflicts in standup meetings.

    View Jean-Pierre → Download AgentOS

    The Problem¶

    Dependencies are invisible until they break. Team A is waiting on Team B's API. Team B doesn't know they're blocking anyone. By the time it surfaces in standup, you've lost a sprint. Dependency management is the #1 hidden risk in multi-project organizations.

    The Solution¶

    Dependency Radar makes the invisible visible. Every dependency relationship is mapped, visualized, and monitored. JP highlights high-risk chains — where a delay in one project cascades through three others — and suggests concrete mitigation actions.

    Impact

    Dependency surprises drop to near-zero. You see the risk chain before it breaks, not after.

    What You See¶

    Dependency Map¶

    Visual map of all dependency relationships: which projects depend on which, what issues block what, and where the critical path runs.

    Risk Chains¶

    JP identifies cascading risk chains — when a delay in Project A ripples through Projects B, C, and D. These compound risks are impossible to spot in isolated dashboards.

    Upstream / Downstream¶

    Clear visualization of who depends on you and who you depend on. Know your impact radius before making scheduling decisions.

    Mitigation Actions¶

    JP doesn't just show risks — it suggests concrete actions: "Unblock the Auth API PR to prevent cascading delays in 3 downstream projects."

    Try It¶

    "Show me all cross-project dependencies and flag any high-risk chains"

    → JP maps dependency relationships across all tracked projects → highlights risk chains and suggests actions. ⏱️ ~10 seconds.
