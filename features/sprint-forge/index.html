    Sprint Forge¶

    Plan smarter sprints in minutes, not hours. Jean-Pierre pulls live backlog data from Jira and GitHub, analyzes your team's real velocity, and generates optimized sprint plans — so you can stop debating scope and start shipping.

    View Jean-Pierre → Download AgentOS

    The Problem¶

    Sprint planning is a 2-hour ritual that drains everyone. Half the time is spent gathering data — who's available, what's in the backlog, what carried over from last sprint. The other half is spent debating scope with incomplete information.

    The Solution¶

    Sprint Forge does the prep work in seconds. JP pulls your live backlog, analyzes team velocity trends, flags capacity risks, and presents a prioritized sprint plan — ready for your team to review and refine.

    Impact

    Sprint planning goes from 2 hours to 15 minutes. Walk in with a data-driven plan instead of a blank board.

    What You Get¶

    Velocity-Based Planning¶

    JP knows how fast your team actually delivers — not how fast you hope they do. Sprint scope is calibrated to real historical velocity.

    Smart Prioritization¶

    Backlog items are ranked by impact, dependencies, and risk. No more gut-feel prioritization — every suggestion is data-backed.

    Capacity Alerts¶

    JP flags when a sprint is overloaded, when key contributors are spread too thin, or when dependencies create bottleneck risks — before you commit.

    Interactive Refinement¶

    Adjust scope, reassign tasks, and refine estimates directly in the interface. JP updates the capacity analysis in real-time as you make changes.

    Try It¶

    "Plan the next sprint based on our current backlog and team velocity"

    → JP analyzes backlog, checks velocity, generates a scoped sprint plan with risk flags. ⏱️ ~10 seconds.
