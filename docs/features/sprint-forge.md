## The Problem

Sprint planning is a **2-hour ritual** that drains everyone. Half the time is spent gathering data — who's available, what's in the backlog, what carried over from last sprint. The other half is spent debating scope with incomplete information.

---

## The Solution

<div class="screenshot" markdown>
![Sprint Forge — AI-powered sprint planning and refinement](../assets/screens/flavors/pm/sprint-forge.png)
</div>

Sprint Forge does the prep work **in seconds**. JP pulls your live backlog, analyzes team velocity trends, flags capacity risks, and presents a prioritized sprint plan — ready for your team to review and refine.

    **Sprint planning goes from 2 hours to 15 minutes.** Walk in with a data-driven plan instead of a blank board.
