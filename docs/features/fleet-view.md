## AI-Powered Fleet Intelligence

Fleet View isn't just a list — it's an **intelligent portfolio analyzer**:

- :material-brain: **Cross-project risk analysis** — JP identifies risks that only emerge when looking across projects
- :material-chart-timeline: **Velocity trending** — spot team slowdowns before they impact delivery
- :material-alert: **Proactive alerts** — stale PRs, sprint bottlenecks, and dependency conflicts surfaced automatically
- :material-text-box: **One-click reports** — generate portfolio-wide status reports for leadership in seconds
