    Meeting Notes & Knowledge¶

    Never lose a decision or action item again. JP generates structured meeting minutes, tracks action items, and builds a knowledge graph that connects insights across all your meetings — so the important stuff never falls through the cracks.

    View Jean-Pierre → Download AgentOS

    The Problem¶

    Meeting notes live in a Google Doc nobody reads. Action items get discussed but never tracked. Three weeks later, someone asks "Didn't we decide on that already?" and nobody remembers. The knowledge generated in meetings evaporates.

    The Solution¶

    Tell JP what happened and he structures it automatically — every decision captured, every action item tracked with an owner, every follow-up queued. Over time, JP builds a knowledge graph from your meeting history, connecting topics, decisions, and people across meetings.

    Impact

    Every meeting produces a permanent, searchable artifact. Action items get tracked. Decisions don't need to be re-made. Knowledge compounds.

    What You Get¶

    Structured Minutes¶

    AI-generated meeting minutes with attendees, key discussion points, decisions, and action items — formatted and ready to share.

    Action Tracking¶

    Every action item has an owner, deadline, and status. Review all open action items across all meetings with one command.

    Searchable History¶

    Full meeting history, indexed and searchable. "What did we decide about the API redesign?" — JP finds the exact meeting and decision.

    Knowledge Graph¶

    JP extracts entities, decisions, and relationships from your meetings and builds a visual knowledge graph — connecting people, topics, and outcomes.

    Try It¶

    "Create a meeting note: Design review with Alice and Bob. We decided to go with the new API v3 approach. Action: Alice to create the migration plan by Friday."

    → JP creates structured minutes, files the decision, creates a tracked action item for Alice. ⏱️ ~5 seconds.

    "Show all open action items from my meetings"

    → JP surfaces every unresolved action item across all meetings, grouped by deadline. ⏱️ ~3 seconds.
