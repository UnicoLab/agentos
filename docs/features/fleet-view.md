# Fleet View

Monitor all your projects at once — a multi-project health overview ranked by risk score.

---

## Overview

Fleet View gives managers a bird's-eye view of **all tracked projects**. Each project shows:

- **Health Score** — AI-calculated from PR activity, sprint progress, and team velocity
- **Risk Level** — Color-coded: 🟢 Low · 🟡 Medium · 🔴 High
- **Key Metrics** — Open PRs, active sprint items, recent commits
- **Quick Actions** — Jump to dashboard, ask JP, or trigger a report

---

## Use Cases

- **Engineering Managers** — Monitor 5–20 repos across teams at a glance
- **CTOs** — Executive overview of all active projects
- **Tech Leads** — Identify which projects need attention this week
- **Freelancers** — Manage multiple client projects from one hub

---

## Accessing Fleet View

Click **Fleet** in the sidebar, or press ++cmd+k++ and type "fleet".
