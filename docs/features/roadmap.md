## The Problem

Your roadmap lives in a slide deck that's already outdated. Updating it means manually checking Jira, cross-referencing sprint dates, and adjusting milestones — a chore that takes hours and is outdated again by next week.

---

## The Solution

<div class="screenshot" markdown>
![Roadmap — Visual project timeline with milestones](../assets/screens/flavors/pm/roadmap.png)
</div>

JP generates and maintains your roadmap **from live data**. Milestones come from Jira epics and release targets. Timeline adjustments happen automatically as sprint data evolves. Share it with stakeholders knowing it reflects reality — not last month's optimism.

!!! success "Impact"
    **Your roadmap is always current.** No more "let me update that deck" — it's already up to date.
